    • About

    Alex O'Connor, known as Rex Orange County, is a talented singer and songwriter celebrated for his unique bedroom pop style, which blends elements of jazz, hip-hop, and soul. His musical journey began in 2015 when he gained online recognition for his self-produced album "bcos u will never b free." With subsequent releases like "Apricot Princess" in 2017 and "PONY" in 2019, he further expanded his musical horizons, earning broader acclaim. In 2022, he released his fourth album, "Who Cares?," featuring a collaboration with rapper Tyler, The Creator. Rex Orange County's music is characterized by its literary and quirky qualities, setting him apart as a prominent figure in the contemporary music scene.
